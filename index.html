Our Story

Crafted from
passion & precision

MAREN began as a single table in a borrowed kitchen. Today it stands as one of New York's most celebrated tasting destinations — earning two Michelin stars and a reputation built on obsessive sourcing, bold technique, and quiet hospitality.

Every plate is a conversation between season, soil, and memory. We work with fewer than twenty farms and change our menu entirely with each harvest.
